Who are we?

Dum Dum Kishore Bharati High School is a boys only school in Dum Dum. This school is affiliated to the West Bengal Board of Secondary Education for 10th Board exam (Madhyamik Pariksha/Secondary Examination) and West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education for the 12th Board exam (Uchcha Madhyamik/Higher Secondary Examination). Dum Dum Kishore Bharati School was established by Mr. Mihir Sengupta on 16 January 1965. Since then, Kishore Bharati has walked a long way crossing multiple hurdles, and recently completed its Golden Jubilee year. From Drama to scientific competitions, the students of the school participate widely and won numerous awards. The alumnus of the school are spread all over the globe holding eminent positions in Science, Technology, Literature and other sectors.
